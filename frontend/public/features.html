Features

NeoGarden focuses on practical landscaping: fast layout, realistic previews, and easy save/load.

Immersive 3D Preview

See scale and depth with lighting and shadow previews. Rotate and examine plant groupings before planting.

Smart Save & Restore

Multiple saved designs, Save As, Rename and Delete. Export JSON for sharing.

Intuitive Land Drawing

Click points to define boundaries. The editor smooths curves and computes area automatically.

Plant Library & Spacing

A curated plant library with size hints and spacing helpers. Place trees, shrubs, and groundcovers easily.

Light & Mood Preview

Toggle day-night preview to check how planting will look under different lighting conditions.
